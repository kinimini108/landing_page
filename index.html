The Allure of Aquamarine

Named after seawater aquamarine's fresh watery hue is a cool plunge into a refreshing pool.

Gemstones: Aquamarine is a gemstone known for its beautiful blue color, ranging from pale blue to a deeper, more intense blue. It belongs to the beryl family of minerals and is treasured for its serene and calming hues. The aquamarine gemstones used in these earrings are carefully selected for their quality, color, and clarity.
